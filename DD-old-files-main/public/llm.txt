## About Dumpster Diverz

Dumpster Diverz is a locally-owned waste management company providing eco-friendly trash services to residential, commercial, and HOA customers throughout Northern Colorado. We specialize in weekly trash pickup, recycling services, commercial dumpsters, and roll-off container rentals.
